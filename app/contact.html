What to Expect When You Contact Us

  • Adoption Inquiries: We'll send you an application and schedule a home visit (in Casablanca, Rabat, or Marrakech areas)
  • Surrender Requests: We conduct a compassionate intake interview and may arrange pickup in major cities
  • Volunteer Applications: We'll send you our volunteer packet and schedule orientation at nearest center
  • Stray Reports: Our rescue team will assess and coordinate with local volunteers
  • Follow-up: We maintain contact throughout the process to ensure the best outcome

Specific Information

  • We work with local municipalities across Morocco for stray cat management
  • All cats are vaccinated and microchipped according to Moroccan veterinary standards
  • We provide adoption contracts in both French and Arabic
  • Home checks are conducted to ensure safe environments for cats in Morocco
  • We collaborate with Moroccan veterinary schools for spay/neuter programs

Thank you for considering supporting our mission in Morocco. Every message, call, and inquiry helps us save more cats and find them loving homes across the country.
