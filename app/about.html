About Our Mission

Finding Forever Homes for Cats in Need

At Cats Gallery, we are more than just a website showcasing adorable cats. We are a volunteer-driven organization dedicated to rescuing cats without homes and helping those whose owners can no longer care for them, finding each feline a loving forever home.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Cats Gallery began as a small group of cat lovers who noticed the growing number of cats in our community without homes. What started as a simple effort to help a few local cats has grown into a comprehensive adoption platform connecting hundreds of cats with caring families.

Our Volunteer Work

Our dedicated team of volunteers works tirelessly to:

  • Rescue stray and abandoned cats from the streets
  • Provide temporary foster care for cats in transition
  • Assist families who can no longer care for their cats due to various circumstances
  • Screen potential adopters to ensure safe, loving environments
  • Provide medical care and rehabilitation for cats in need
  • Educate the community about responsible pet ownership

How Our Platform Helps

This website serves as the heart of our operation, allowing us to:

  • Showcase Available Cats: Each cat gets a detailed profile with photos, personality traits, and care requirements
  • Connect with Adopters: Potential adopters can browse, search, and filter to find their perfect match
  • Share Success Stories: We celebrate every successful adoption with updates from happy families
  • Educate the Public: Providing resources on cat care, behavior, and responsible ownership
  • Coordinate Volunteers: Managing our network of fosters, transporters, and caregivers

Types of Cats We Help

We assist a diverse range of cats, including:

  • Stray Cats: Found living on the streets without homes
  • Abandoned Cats: Left behind by previous owners
  • Owner Surrenders: Cats from families facing difficult circumstances
  • Special Needs Cats: Those requiring extra medical care or attention
  • Senior Cats: Older cats seeking peaceful retirement homes
  • Kittens: Young cats needing nurturing environments

Need to Surrender a Cat?

If you can no longer care for your cat, we're here to help. We provide a safe, compassionate process to ensure your cat finds a new loving home.

We work with you to understand your situation and find the best solution for both you and your cat.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Never euthanizing healthy or treatable cats
  • Providing comprehensive medical care to all cats in our program
  • Thoroughly screening all potential adopters
  • Offering lifelong support to adopters
  • Operating with transparency and integrity
